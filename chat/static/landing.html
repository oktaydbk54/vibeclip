FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need to install anything?
No. VibeClip runs entirely in your browser — open a tab, drop in a video, and start editing by chat. No app, no GPU, no render farm.
How does “editing by talking” actually work?
You describe the edit in plain words — “cut the silences,” “add captions,” “make it punchier.” VibeClip runs the matching pipeline and stages a before / after so you approve the result before it sticks.
Can I undo an edit I don't like?
Always. Every edit is proposed as a B version next to your current A. Keep it or discard it — nothing is destructive, and the full history is reversible.
What kind of videos work best?
Talking-head footage, podcasts, streams, lectures, interviews — any long landscape recording you want to turn into vertical 9:16 shorts. Smart reframe keeps the speaker centered as it crops.
How much does it cost?
Free during early access — no card required. We're still shaping our paid plans and will give you ample notice before anything changes.
Can I export in 1080p without a watermark?
Yes — high-resolution, clean exports are part of what we're building. Specifics will firm up alongside our pricing; for now, early-access creators get the full studio.
